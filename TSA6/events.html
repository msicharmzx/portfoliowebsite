MLBB × Jujutsu Kaisen is a collaboration between Mobile Legends: Bang Bang and Jujutsu Kaisen (Japanese: 呪術廻戦). The event first debuted on 18 February 2023. Yin "Yuji Itadori", Julian "Megumi Fushiguro", Melissa "Nobara Kugisaki", and Xavier "Satoru Gojo" - the four Jujutsu Sorcerers.

MLBB Kung Fu Panda skins collaboration will feature three new exclusive skins: General Kai Thamuz, Kung Fu Panda Akai, and Lord Shen Ling. Other rewards such as avatar border, recall, and other in-game cosmetics are up for grabs for those who would like to participate in the event starting this weekend.
